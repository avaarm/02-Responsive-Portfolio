About Me

Hello, my name is Armenuhi. I love technology and science. I work in pre-Clinical research primarily on Hematopoetic Stem Cells and CAR T cells. I am currently attending the UW Coding Bootcamp and love my classes and classmates. With the knowledge and experties I gain from this bootcamp I will start a non-profit that helps Armenian women who want to pursue careers in technology.

When I'm not busy researching or studying for my classes, I love to do energy bar samplings for a company called, Ritual Energy. I also love volunteering and for the past three years I was a head clinical coordinator at a free clinic in Tijuana, Mexico.
